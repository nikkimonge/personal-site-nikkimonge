How I Came To Love Taylor Swift

June 15, 2020

My two older sisters went to Taylor Swift's 1989 Concert without me. I was jealous. My cousin happened to have two extra tickets and gave them to my 2 sisters because they were fans. I was never invited. I was sad. My sisters said it was because I didn't listen to Taylor's music. They were fans, and I was just a "local." I've listened to her music on the radio and whenever my sisters played it, but other than that I never really listened to her music on my own. To be sure I would get an invite next time, I listened to Taylor's music on repeat for months. I did my research on her. I invested so much time learning about Taylor and figuring out who she is. So when the album "Reputation" came out, I was a huge fan. I spent the next 6 months listening to the album on repeat, memorizing lyrics and relating to the songs. It wasn't until Christmas when I learned that I was, finally, invited to the Reputation concert. Since then, I've loved her music and memorized the lyrics to all 7 of her albums.
